Features

Built for the real world.

⚡
Real-time alerts
Push any of 8 alert types to every device in the building in under 100ms — from any browser, any device, anywhere.
🗺️
Building floor map
Visual map of your campus with live device status — green for online, red for alert active, gray for offline.
📅
Bell schedule management
Set and sync bell schedules for every room and every campus. Updates push to all devices instantly.
🎨
Custom branding
School name, logo, colors, and motto — customized per room or building-wide in minutes.
📱
Remote MDM
Update, reboot, and configure any device remotely. OTA updates roll out overnight without IT touching a single unit.
🔒
16 screen states
Idle, alert, Sub Mode, countdown, announcement, passing period, PA broadcast, offline — every scenario handled.
