Why Halo

Everything the old clock
should have been.

Halo mounts where your existing clock is. One ethernet cable. No electrician. Up in 30 minutes.

◎
Circular display
Signature circular IPS touchscreen — visually distinct from every other display on the wall. Students and staff know immediately what it is.
◉
LED ring alerts
RGB LED ring around the entire display face. Red for fire, dark blue for lockdown, amber for hold — visible from the hallway without reading the screen.
⏰
Live bell schedule
Current period, time remaining, and next bell displayed at all times. Syncs with your district's bell schedule automatically.
📡
Instant alerts
Full-screen color-coded emergency alerts pushed from the ClockOS dashboard reach every Halo in the building in under 100ms.
🔊
Built-in speaker
PA-quality speaker for audio announcements and alert tones — no separate intercom speaker needed.
⚡
PoE powered
Single ethernet cable provides both power and connectivity. No power outlet needed at the clock location.

What Halo is not

Clear boundaries.

✕Not a surveillance device. Halo does not record audio or video.
✕Not a general tablet. ClockOS runs in locked kiosk mode.
✕Not a phone system replacement — it complements existing PA infrastructure.
✕Not a digital signage board. No ads, no social feeds, no content management.
