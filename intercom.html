Features

Built for the real world.

🎙️
Retractable mic arm
A mic arm deploys from a hole at the top of the unit — visually communicates intercom function, stores flush when not in use.
🔴
Physical Announce button
A dedicated hardware button at the bottom initiates an office call instantly — no app, no login, no delay.
↕️
Portrait orientation
Instantly recognizable as the intercom unit. Visually distinct from every other Kova display in the building.
🔒
Privacy by design
Mic is push-to-talk only. An LED indicator on the mic arm shows active/inactive status. Never always-on.
📡
Full ClockOS
All standard ClockOS features — time, schedule, alerts, Sub Mode, and announcements — in portrait layout.
🔊
Two-way audio
Far-field mic array picks up voice from across the room. Crystal-clear speaker for office announcements.
