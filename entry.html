Features

Built for the real world.

👋
Visitor welcome
Displays school name, today's date, and a directional prompt — Welcome ↑ — guiding visitors to the office without a separate sign.
🪪
Visitor check-in
Tap-to-check-in mode turns Entry into a light visitor management terminal during school hours.
🔒
Emergency lockout
During lockdown, Entry displays a locked state and can integrate with door lock hardware — exterior doors secured from the admin dashboard.
📞
Office intercom
Built-in speaker and optional mic for two-way communication between the entrance and the front office.
◎
Circular form factor
Matches Halo's design language — visitors see the same display family inside the building and at the entrance.
📡
Full ClockOS alerts
All safety alerts push to Entry simultaneously with classroom displays — the entrance is always in sync with the rest of the building.
