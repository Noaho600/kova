Features

Built for the real world.

🚌
Route and stop display
Current route, next stop, and estimated arrival time — always visible to the driver without taking their eyes off the road.
👥
Live student headcount
Integrates with Kova Check to show real-time boarding count. Driver always knows who's on the bus.
📡
ClockOS safety alerts
Receives the same lockdown and emergency alerts as classroom displays — drivers know immediately when school status changes.
📞
Dispatch communication
One-tap communication to district dispatch. Driver can report issues without using a personal device.
🛡️
Ruggedized housing
Vibration-resistant, temperature-rated enclosure built for bus environments. IP54 rated.
📶
Cellular + WiFi
Stays connected on route via 4G LTE. Falls back to WiFi when at school.
