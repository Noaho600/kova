How it works

Up and running in a day.

No new wiring. No IT contractor. Mounts where your existing clock is.

01
🔧
Mount & connect
Kova mounts directly where your existing clock is. One ethernet cable for power and data via PoE — no electrician needed.
02
⚙️
Configure in ClockOS
Add your school name, logo, colors, and bell schedule in the web-based admin dashboard. Takes about 10 minutes per building.
03
📡
Manage remotely
Push alerts, update schedules, and monitor every device from anywhere — no physical access required. Works from phone or desktop.
04
🔔
Alert in one tap
One click in the dashboard sends a full-screen color alert to every Kova device in the building — or target specific rooms and floors.
