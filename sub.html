Features

Built for the real world.

👤
Seating chart with names
Student names and photos arranged in the actual classroom layout. Sub matches faces to names instantly — no introductions needed.
📋
Step-by-step lesson plan
The teacher's lesson broken into numbered steps. Current step highlighted. Sub taps to mark complete as the period progresses.
⚠️
Behavior and support flags
Discrete flags for students with behavior notes or accommodations — visible only when the sub taps the student's name.
🚨
Emergency procedures
Fire exit route, lockdown instructions, office extension, and neighboring teacher contact — always one tap away.
📊
End-of-day summary
Sub logs which lesson steps were completed and any incidents. Teacher receives a full summary automatically.
✅
Attendance tracking
Sub marks absent students directly on the seating chart. Syncs to your SIS automatically.
