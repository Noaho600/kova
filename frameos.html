What FrameOS does

Built for the teacher.
Seen by every student.

FrameOS turns the Kova Frame into the smartest display in the classroom — and the most important safety screen in the building.

👤
Sub Mode
When a substitute is assigned, FrameOS activates Sub Mode — showing the seating chart with student photos, step-by-step lesson plan, behavior notes, and emergency procedures. Everything a sub needs, nothing they have to hunt for.
📺
HDMI lesson display
Teachers plug in their laptop via HDMI. FrameOS shows lesson content full-screen while keeping a persistent ClockOS sidebar showing the time, period, and alert status.
🚨
Full-screen emergency override
When a ClockOS alert is pushed, FrameOS immediately overrides everything — lesson content, Sub Mode, whatever is showing — with the full-screen color alert. No alert goes unseen in the room.
✏️
Touch + stylus input
Full 10-point touch and active stylus support. Teachers annotate lessons, students interact with content, subs tap through the lesson plan. FrameOS manages all input states.
🔗
ClockOS sync
FrameOS stays in constant sync with ClockOS — bell schedule, alert states, announcements, and period information all flow automatically from the same server that runs every Halo and Beacon in the building.
📋
Attendance + incident log
In Sub Mode, subs mark attendance directly on the seating chart and log incidents. Teacher receives a full day summary automatically — the sub day is never a black hole of lost instruction.
FrameOS vs ClockOS

Different screens.
Different jobs.

FrameOS and ClockOS are two separate operating systems designed for two very different roles in the classroom.

FrameOS
Kova Frame · front of classroom · teacher-facing
✓55"–75" full touchscreen smart board
✓Sub Mode with seating chart + lesson plan
✓HDMI lesson content input
✓Stylus + full touch input
✓Full-screen alert override
✓Attendance + incident logging
VS
ClockOS
Halo + Beacon · wall clock · room-facing
✓24" circular wall clock display
✓Real-time time, date, period, bell schedule
✓LED ring ambient alert lighting
✓PA audio announcements
✓Minimal touch — volume + brightness only
✕No Sub Mode — no general touch
